Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about DevConf 2026

What is included in a ticket?

Standard tickets include all 3 conference days, 48 talks, and 2 workshops. Pro tickets add unlimited workshops and a VIP dinner.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes! Every ticket gets access to recorded sessions. Standard gets 30 days of access, Pro gets lifetime access.

Where is the event located?

DevConf 2026 will be held at the Dhaka Convention Hall. Full venue details will be emailed before the event.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a teammate.
